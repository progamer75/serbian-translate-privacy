Privacy Policy for Serbian Translate

Effective date: 07.04.2026

Serbian Translate (“the app”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

The app may collect audio (microphone) and text input for translation.

2. How We Use Data

Audio is processed to convert speech into text.

Text is sent to third-party APIs to provide translation functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses external services for translation, including:

OpenAI

Google (Gemini API)

User input (text and/or audio-derived text) may be transmitted to these services for processing.

We do not control how these third parties handle data. Please refer to their privacy policies:

https://openai.com/privacy

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

We do not store user audio or text on our servers. Data is processed in real-time only.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data with third parties except as required to provide the app’s core functionality (translation).
